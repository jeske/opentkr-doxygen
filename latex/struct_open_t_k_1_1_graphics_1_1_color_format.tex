\subsection{Detailed Description}
Defines the \hyperlink{struct_open_t_k_1_1_graphics_1_1_color_format}{Color\-Format} component of a \hyperlink{class_open_t_k_1_1_graphics_1_1_graphics_mode}{Graphics\-Mode}.
